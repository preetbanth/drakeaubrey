Bio

Aubrey Drake Graham, known professionally as Drake, is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, actor, and entrepreneur. He first gained recognition as an actor on the teen drama television series Degrassi: The Next Generation, before transitioning to music. Drake's music career began in 2006 with the release of his debut mixtape Room for Improvement. He has since released several successful albums, including Scorpion, Views, and More Life.
